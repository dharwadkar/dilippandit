Not all those wander are lost - JRR Tolkien

I am an avid traveler and a hobbyist bird watcher. Wherever I go I try to not just visit the place, but to take in the local culture, customs and of course the local birds. During my travels, I have visited a lot of places, wild life sanctuatries and regions of India.

This section is my attempt to document my travels and recollection. I hope this will help you, the visitor to know the areas better and plan your visits to the region better.
